How It Works

Simple Steps to Access Any Paper
1

Install the Extension

Download and install the Sci-Hub DOI Finder extension from your browser's extension store. It's free and takes just one click.

2

Browse Academic Sites

Visit any academic journal, research database, or paper citation. The extension automatically detects available DOIs on the page.

3

Click to Access

Click on the Sci-Hub DOI Finder icon or button that appears next to the paper. The extension will instantly locate the full text.

4

Download & Read

Download the PDF instantly or read online. Your research just became faster, easier, and completely free of paywalls.
