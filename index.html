FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install the extension?

Simply visit the Chrome Web Store, search for "DOI to Sci-Hub", and click "Add to Chrome". The extension will be installed and ready to use immediately.

Is this extension safe to use?

Yes, our extension only scans for DOI links and adds access buttons. It doesn't collect personal data or modify any webpage content permanently.

Does it work on all websites?

The extension works on any website that contains DOI links, including journal websites, Google Scholar, PubMed, and academic databases.

What if Sci-Hub doesn't have the paper?

The extension checks availability before showing the key icon. If you don't see a red key, it means the paper isn't available on Sci-Hub.

Is this extension free?

Yes, absolutely free! No subscriptions, no hidden costs. We believe in open access to scientific knowledge.

How do I navigate between multiple DOIs?

When multiple DOIs are found, use the floating navigation arrows on the right side of your browser to jump between them easily.
